Konvict - redirect / tracking

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
(Macros)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   - Tracking macro's are in a different format from the normal macros.
   - All tracking macros are in the format of "[name]".
   - Use [fromdomain] for the current domain used when sending, or
     you can optionally use $RandomDomain() in it's place.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
(Format)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Redirect Format:

http://[fromdomain]/[user_id]/[camp_id]/[list_id]/[msg_id]/[subj_id]/

Tracking an open:

http://[fromdomain]/[user_id]/[camp_id]/[list_id]/[msg_id]/[subj_id]/o/
